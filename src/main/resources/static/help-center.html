Quick Help

Most common questions

How do I search buses?

Go to the home page, enter source, destination, and date, then continue to the dedicated results page.

How do I choose a seat?

Pick a schedule first, then select any available seat. The seat page refreshes to reflect live availability.

What payment modes exist?

You can confirm with pay on board or go through the online flow that creates, checks out, and verifies a session.

Can I cancel a booking?

Yes. Logged-in customers can open My Bookings and cancel active bookings, which releases the seat back to the system.
