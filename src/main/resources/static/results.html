Booking for women

Priority support and safety-focused travel for women on every bus.

Know more

We are fully dedicated to every woman passenger's safety from boarding to drop-off.

  • All buses are GPS-tracked, and route movement is monitored by our control team.
  • Drivers and onboard staff are verified and trained for safe, respectful conduct.
  • Priority seat support is available for women during boarding and seat allocation.
  • Emergency escalation support is available through our helpline for quick assistance.
  • Boarding and drop-off points are planned to be as safe and well-lit as possible.

Your trust matters to us. We follow a zero-tolerance policy for harassment and take immediate action on every report.
